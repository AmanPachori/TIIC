OyeOrder Ecommerce Pvt Ltd

Online Food Delivery in Gwalior

OyeOrder is an online food ordering and delivery company based out of Gwalior, India. OyeOrder was inspired by the thought of providing a complete food and beverage ordering and delivery solutions from the Gwalior best Restaurants, Bakery Shops, Sweet shops and Namkeen Shops to our valuable customers. OyeOrder provides you a different flavor of food on a single platform through our website and Android mobile application. We have our own smart delivery team which pickup orders from our vendors and deliver it to customers.
