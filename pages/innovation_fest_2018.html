TIIC Organises an Idea Innovation Challenge: Digital Art and Design of India (DADi-2k18)

In Association with:

DADi-2k18


TIIC is organizing DADi-2k18, a School Idea Innovation Fest-2018 with an objective to provide a platform to present/showcase the innovative ideas/Models of young minds. Students can submit their idea or innovation or product design in this fest and get opportunity to be awarded for pre Incubation under TIIC. TIIC will select maximum up to three innovation for their further development with mentorship and financial support.




    The Government of Madhya Pradesh has laid great emphasis on Innovations in Government departments. Motivated from such Government initiatives at state as well as national level especially through Digital India, Smart City, Startup and Standup India, we have planned to organize anIdea Innovation Challenge for school going students of Madhya Pradesh and other states. Innovation is the buzzword of 21st century. It has revolutionized almost every corner of our surrounding. Innovation isn’t solely represented by new devices, ideas or methods, but also by the process of uncovering new ways to do things. It can also pertain to modifying business models and adapting to changes to achieve better products and services. Innovation is not merely a word or an event. It's an ongoing process. One can innovate only after understanding a problem and try to find out its solution. Innovation and out of the box thinking are the key to realizing the New India and a concept behind this event DADi-2l18.


    Objective:

    The main objective of this challenge is to motivate school going students to showcase the Invention, creativity and resourcefulness and to think out of box and contribute towards innovation which will help the nation at large. The Innovation Festival is open ended, encouraging and generous in Spirit. It is about participation and not competition. It is a unique initiative of the TIIC-ABV IIITM, Gwalior to recognize, respect and reward grassroots innovations/ideas and is based on the theme “Smart Citizen for Smart City”.

    Area for Idea/Innovation submission

  • Agriculture
  • Health
  • Education
  • Energy
  • Environment
  • Social
  • Business
  • Sports
  • Any other you feel fit for the society


Who can participate?

TIIC will offer cash prize, certificate of merit, medal, goodies and opportunity to work with TIIC as a member of Raman Innovation Club. Any School student studying in 6 to 11 standard in any school of Madhya Pradesh, who has any idea/innovation worth for the smarter citizen of India


How to Register?


Fill the form using the button below and confirm your participation. The last date to confirm your participation is 10th October 2018. 18th October 2018 In case of any difficulty in online submission, you may send a one page abstract of your idea/innovation

When and where?

The contest will be on 26-27 October, 2018 in IIITM Campus.

Rewards & Recognition

An expert committee will evaluate and select maximum upto 3 innovations in each category [(6-8) and (9-11)] for the award (cash, certificate, medal, gift and opportunbity to work with Raman Innovation Club of TIIC) and a further development of idea/innovation with mentoring and financial support.
