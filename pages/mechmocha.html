MechMocha Game Studios

Casual mobile games development studio

Mech Mocha Game Studios is an independent game development studio based in India. We are a small team of game developers who work as hard as Mechs and love having Mocha and that’s why we are called Mech Mocha.Founded in November 2011, Mech Mocha is incubated and supported by Technology Innovation and Incubation Center(TIIC), Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior, India.

We are currently working on our iOS game “Pablo and The Puppet Punch” and we need a lot of support from everyone in the game community. We are doing our bit by sharing our development updates and solutions to problems we face during development continuously on our blog.Check it out here: Our Blog
