DADi-2k18

TIIC is organizing School Innovation Fest-2018 to provide the platform to showcase the idea/Models. Students can submit their idea or innovation or product design in this fest and get opportunity to be awarded for pre Incubation under TIIC. TIIC will select maximum up to three innovation for their further development with mentorship and financial support.


Area for Idea/Innovation submission

  • Agriculture
  • Health
  • Education
  • Energy
  • Environment
  • Social
  • Business
  • Sports
  • Any other you feel fit for the society

Who can participate?

Any School student studying in 6 to 11 standard in any school of Madhya Pradesh, who has any idea/innovation worth for the smarter citizen of India.


How to Register

Fill the form using the button below and confirm your participation. The last date to confirm your participation is 10th October 2018. In case of any difficulty in online submission, you may send a one page abstract of your idea/innovation


When and where?

The contest will be on 26-27 October, 2018 in IIITM Campus.


Rewards & Recognition

An expert committee will evaluate and select maximum upto 3 innovations in each category [(6-8) and (9-11)] for the award (cash, certificate, medal, gift and opportunbity to work with Raman Innovation Club of TIIC) and a further development of idea/innovation with mentoring and financial support.
