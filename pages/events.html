TIIC



Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre is the Industrial Interface of Atal Bihari Vajpayee-Indian Institute of Information Technology & Management, Gwalior established in 2008. A registered society under MP Act 1973. This centre has been set up due the initiatives taken by IIITM faculty,students and strong alumni base.

Events under TIIC

The event has provided a great platform to the researchers to interact under one umbrella for further development of efficient and secure information processing technologies. Workshop on Enterprise Healthcare Systems & Solutions (Online mode)

Date - 15th -19th January 2022

Venue - Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre ABV- IIITM Gwalior


The Enterprise Health System & Solution (EHSS) comprises of Market Development, Operations, Medical Data Acquisition, Electronic Health Record Generation and Health Care Management. EHSS gives health plan customers access to key partners in the market. The EHSS Platform applications and tools offer capabilities such as managing Electronic Health Record from medical data acquisition through enrolment and provides data security and clinical decision support. The workshop will provide sessions, discussion and presentation on future research directions

Innovation in Design and Development of Hybrid Antennas for 5G Communication (Online mode)

Date - 15th -19th January 2022

Venue - Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre ABV- IIITM Gwalior


This FDP aims at providing strong theoretical and practical experience in the field of 5G Communication. In 5G Hybrid Antenna Design; leaders in the field describe state-of-the-art research in applying the latest methodologies in the context of tomorrow’s most important 5G applications, ranging from next-generation cellular telephony and personal communication services,. Representatives of industries and academia have started to look into the echnological developments toward the next generation (5G), as the rollout of 4G mobile communication networks take place. mm wave communication, Software Defined Radio, HetNets, algorithms and architectures have an increasing important role to play in meeting the central challenges faced in the design of advanced wireless communication systems.

Workshop on Enterprise Application Development, Reliability, and Security (Online mode)

Date - 5th -9th February 2022

Venue - Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre ABV- IIITM Gwalior


With the recent growth in the field of wireless communication, number of wireless based application and services have emerged comprising large number of devices. In this context, Internet of Things (IoT) is one of the emerging applications which allow devices and objects get smarter by connecting them to the internet. IoT has wide range of application starting from industry, health to environment monitoring. This workshop will provide an opportunity to the participants to learn fundamentals of IoT, design standards and its challenges. Some of the topics which will be covered in this workshop include security issues in IoT and their countermeasures, IoT in communication, use of ML in IoT, and real-life use case of IoT. The aim of this workshop is to appraise the knowledge of faculty member, research scholar and UG/PG students with current state of the art technologies in IOT and challenges.

Technological Challenges in Digital Financial Inclusion

Date - 20-21 April 20232

Venue - Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre ABV- IIITM Gwalior


The growth of digital payments in India and the availability of various easy and convenient digital payment solutions have facilitated ease of living citizens, financial inclusion, and growth of business and economy. During the pandemic, the availability of contactless digital payment solutions such as BHIM-UPI facilitated social distancing and continuity of businesses, including small merchants. The benefits of using digital payments are an instant and convenient mode of payment, enhanced financial inclusion, increased transparency in the government system, improved speed and timely delivery, National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) system, Bharat Bill Payment System, Enhanced Credit Access, Safe and secure, and so on.

Training team - The members of the training team were Dr. Sunil Kumar, Dr. Mahendra Shukla, Dr. Anuja Dixit, and Dr. Anjali


Applications of IoT Technology in Agriculture

Date - 12-14 May 2023

Venue - Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre ABV- IIITM Gwalior


This workshop aims to provide a comprehensive overview of IoT applications in agriculture, highlighting the potential benefits, challenges, and best practices. The workshop will be suitable for researchers, policymakers, and technology providers who are interested in exploring the latest trends and innovations in IoT-enabled farming. Workshop Objectives was:  Introduce the concept of IoT and its relevance to agriculture.  Highlight the benefits of IoT in agriculture, such as precision farming, resource optimization, and data-driven decision-making.  Discuss the challenges and limitations of IoT adoption in agriculture, such as data privacy, cybersecurity, and interoperability.  Showcase real-world examples of IoT applications in agriculture, such as soil monitoring, weather forecasting, crop management, and livestock tracking.
