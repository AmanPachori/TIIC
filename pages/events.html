Events under TIIC

Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre, ABV-IIITM, Gwalior has organized two days national conference on “Innovation and Entrepreneurship” on February 27-28, 2021. The event has provided a great platform to the researchers to interact under one umbrella for further development of efficient and secure information processing technologies. Efficient and secure data sensing, storage, and processing play pivotal roles in current information age. Total 286 participants have been participated in this online conference and 16 area experts have delivered the talk.

Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre (TIIC) at Atal Bihari Vajpayee-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior has successfully organized the National level Business Plan Competition “VICHAT HAAT” on April 25, 2021. This Vichaar Haat, was the flagship Business-Plan competition of ABV-IIITM Gwalior in association with the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology, Government of India. This competition has served as one of the finest platforms for all the budding entrepreneurs to showcase & get their ideas validated by some of the best & experienced industry experts
