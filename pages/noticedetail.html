Technology Innovation and Incubation Centre, ABV-IIITM Gwalior is organizing a Web Development and Logo Design Contest. For this contest, you'll have to develop a front end template for TIIC's website (www.tiiciiitm.com) and Logo for TIIC. You will have to develop a template for the home page and preferably a couple of other pages as mentioned in the navigation.
Deadline:- July 11, 11:59 PM
Winner's template will be hosted on the website and will receive a cash prize of Rs.2500/- for each.
Instructions:-
1. Develop the template and upload it on github. and e-mail the logo at tiiciiitmg@gmail.com
2. Your repository's README.md should contain screenshot of your template and also instructions to download and use.
3. After uploading your template on github, drop a mail to tiiciiitmg@gmail.com with your repository's link and your name and roll number.


Note:- Only top 20 entries will be considered on the basis of first come first serve.
