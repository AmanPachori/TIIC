Entrepreneurship Development Programme

One Week Entrepreneurship Development Programme From 6th Aug - 10th Aug , 2018

This Entrepreneurship Development Programme (EDP) focuses on developing the entrepreneurial abilities with technological developments which is useful for students/Faculty/consultants from different disciplines.

Theme

This programme will train, develop and motivate the participants to take up Entrepreneurship as a career with requisite skills. This programme will also train them to use the teaching tools and methodologies relevant to entrepreneurship education along with case studies.
