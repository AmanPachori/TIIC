FoodZamo

Exclusive app for restaurants in Gwalior

Local App for every City regarding restaurants/bakeries etc. Home Delivery of food available through the app, Cashback on the table bills, Promocodes for FREE Movie Tickets, Shopping, Footwear, Coffee Shops and promo codes for seasonal offers/events within the City. Promo Codes are given to those customers those who make bills at the restaurants tied up with FoodZamo.

Features of the App:

  • CASHBACK, WALLET ON TABLE BILLS: Cashback of minimum 10% is given to the customer on every table bill. This cashback amount are credited in his app wallet. When the total cashback amount is beyond the threshold limit (Rs.300/- after several visits), then the customer can get a FREE food coupon in the particular restaurant. This scheme is able to make huge profits for the restaurants. So FoodZamo can also get commission from this. Further they can add money to the wallet so that they can use for the next time.


  • HOME DELIVERY: Home Delivery is available through the app where customer can see the menu of a particular restaurant and order his food.


  • PROMOCODES: Promocodes are given monthly once/twice depending upon the situation. If a customer makes a bill of minimum amount of Rs.500/- at restaurant then he/she is able to get a FREE Movie ticket + Discounts on Shopping + Discounts on Footwear + Discounts on Coffee Shops/Hair Salons and many more offers. This makes every person in the city to go to restaurant for the offers which makes huge profits. How our Idea is different from existing food apps? Today in the software field there are many food apps which are providing home delivery of food. But no food app is focusing on table bills which is major part of income for every restaurant. And we are also providing wallet facility at every restaurant. Additionally we are providing promocodes for movie tickets, shopping, footwear, hair salons, beauty parlours etc. So our target is to generate more income to the restaurant, in return we get commissions from them.
